Who are YoHa?

The group YoHa consists mainly of the artists; Graham Harwood and Matsuko Yokokoji


Both have worked on several projects together and previously collaborated under the name: Mongrel


Graham Harwood

Graham has worked on several noteworthy projects including Aluminum, Rehearsal of Memory and the Transmediale Award winning work: Tantalum Memorial.


Matsuko Yokokoji

While having worked with Graham on many projects, Mastuko feelanced as a graphic designer and was the art director for Imperial Enterprise Inc.
